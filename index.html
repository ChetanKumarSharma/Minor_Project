Why Clean Air, Water & Healthy Land Matter

Air

Clean air is essential for human health, supporting respiration and reducing respiratory diseases. It also regulates climate and supports plant growth.

Water

Safe, abundant water is the backbone of ecosystems, agriculture and industry. Protecting water quality prevents disease and sustains biodiversity.

Land

Healthy soils store carbon, grow our food and filter water. Land conservation prevents erosion, maintains habitats and combats desertification.

India’s Environmental Challenges

Rapid urbanization, population growth and industrialization have placed enormous pressure on India’s natural resources. Key challenges include:

  • Air pollution in major cities, driven by vehicles, industry and crop burning
  • Water scarcity and contamination of rivers and groundwater
  • Land degradation from overgrazing, deforestation and unsustainable agriculture
  • Waste management hurdles and plastic pollution
  • Vulnerability to climate change impacts—extreme heat, monsoon variability and sea-level rise

Through data-driven insights and community action, we can tackle these issues and move toward a sustainable future.
